Welcome to Excalibur.

Plugin For name, and stat changing in Minecraft, Eventual goal: To completely replace the exsisting enchantment table with better built enchantment and naming sceme for weaps an tools.

Example: Forge Diamond sword, use new scheme to add name "epic" name BANE OF CREEPERS to sword. Example2: Add possible messaging to sword when used in battle, IE. PLAYER was killed with BANE OF CREEPERS by PLAYER
